Purpose & L1 Boundary

This checklist is for Level 1 engineers performing physical walkthrough inspection in a GB200/Core POD data hall. The objective is to detect visible hardware, power, cooling, cabling, environmental, safety, and housekeeping abnormalities early.

L1 engineers shall observe, record, raise tickets, and escalate. Intrusive actions such as reseating modules, moving fibers, touching liquid cooling connections, or manipulating power cables require an approved MOP and authorization from the shift lead/L2.

L1 Engineer — Permitted vs Prohibited Actions

✓ May Do

✓Perform visual inspection during assigned rounds
✓Take photo evidence if site policy allows
✓Record LED / alarm status without touching equipment
✓Confirm alarm with NOC / monitoring team
✓Raise ticket for all defects
✓Close rack door only if no obstruction and permitted
✓Remove loose packaging from walkway only if safe and permitted

✗ Must Not Do

✗Do not unplug, reseat, or transfer power cable
✗Do not reseat server, GPU tray, switch, PSU, fan, or module
✗Do not touch liquid cooling hose, manifold, or fitting
✗Do not silence CDU / PDU alarm without approval
✗Do not move fiber cable without approved MOP
✗Do not bypass access or escort process
✗Do not acknowledge major alarm without escalation
